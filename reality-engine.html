Why it is different

Reality Engine is not a dashboard.

Most analytics platforms tell you what happened. Reality Engine goes further.

Upload a CSV or Excel file and the platform automatically investigates the data, identifies hidden trends, uncovers risks, discovers opportunities, forecasts future outcomes, and recommends strategic actions.

Instead of requiring users to build dashboards, write SQL queries, or manually search for insights, Reality Engine acts like an intelligent team of analysts working behind the scenes.

Case study

Building a decision intelligence product

Problem

Dashboards often wait for users to know the right question. Many teams need the system to investigate data and surface decisions automatically.

Solution

Reality Engine analyzes uploaded datasets, discovers what matters, predicts outcomes, simulates choices, and recommends actions.

Features

Autonomous discovery, AI investigation, relationship maps, forecasting, simulations, opportunity scanning, warnings, assistant, and reporting.

Tech Stack

React, Tailwind CSS, Chart.js, JavaScript, CSV/XLSX parsing, local processing, Apps Script path, PostgreSQL path, OpenRouter path.

Business Impact

Reality Engine compresses analyst-style investigation into a product experience that helps leaders move from information to action faster.

What I Learned

I learned to design analytics around decisions, not charts, and to connect data engineering, product thinking, and business analysis into one experience.

Future Roadmap

Authentication, persistent workspaces, multi-agent AI, knowledge graphs, external data connectors, deeper forecasting, and autonomous research agents.
