Project Structure

Business Problem

A technical team needs a fast way to understand traffic flow and identify the busiest hosts in a packet capture.

Dataset Overview

The provided logs include broadcast discovery, ARP lookups, ICMP replies, TCP sessions, DNS requests, and TLS traffic.

Solution Approach

I summarized protocols, monitored active hosts, and displayed packet flow in a Cisco/Wireshark inspired layout.

Technologies Used

Wireshark concepts, packet analysis, IP review, device monitoring, CSV data, and troubleshooting workflow.
