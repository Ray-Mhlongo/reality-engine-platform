Transport operations reporting and performance monitoring

Insight Rides

Operational visibility for school transport records, payments, routes, vehicles, and expenses.

Insight Rides organizes transport activity into reporting views that help operators monitor route activity, payment exceptions, vehicle usage, expenses, and business performance.

Project Overview

This project addresses the challenge of monitoring school transport operations when student, route, trip, vehicle, payment, income, and expense records are managed separately.

The solution organizes operational records into dashboards and exception views for payment follow-up, route pressure, cost awareness, and day-to-day decision support.

The business outcome is improved visibility into cash flow, route activity, unpaid accounts, vehicle usage, fuel cost signals, and trip volume.

Business Challenge

Small transport operators need to monitor students, trips, routes, vehicles, payments, and expenses, but fragmented records make it hard to identify overdue payments, high-cost routes, missed activity, or operational pressure.

A consolidated reporting process was needed to reduce manual checking and improve operational decision making.

Data Sources

The project structures data for students, routes, trips, vehicles, payments, income, expenses, alerts, and location context.

These records support payment monitoring, route analysis, vehicle tracking, expense review, and operational performance reporting.

Data Processing and Analysis

I structured JSON based records and designed dashboard views that connect route activity, payment status, vehicle information, and expense records.

The analysis supports exception reporting for missed trips, unpaid students, route pressure, and cost signals.

Tools and Technologies

React, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Chart.js, Leaflet Maps, Local Storage, JSON, dashboard design, location intelligence.

Reporting and Insights

  • Transport operations need linked data across students, routes, trips, payments, and expenses.
  • Cash flow visibility improves when unpaid accounts and trip income are visible in one place.
  • Route and fuel context can reveal operating pressure before it becomes a larger business issue.
  • Alerts help operators focus attention on missed trips, unpaid students, and high cost signals.
