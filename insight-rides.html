Transportation analytics platform

Insight Rides

A management and analytics platform for school transport operations.

Insight Rides gives transport operators visibility into trips, payments, routes, vehicles, expenses, location context, and business performance.

Project Overview

Business problem: Small transport operators often manage students, routes, vehicles, trips, payments, and expenses across separate records. This makes it hard to understand outstanding payments, route pressure, operating cost, and profit visibility.

Objective: Build a platform that organizes transport data into one operating view and helps owners monitor income, costs, route activity, alerts, and business performance.

Methodology: I structured JSON based records for students, routes, trips, vehicles, payments, income, and expenses, then designed dashboard views and alerts for operational decision making.

Outcome: The product turns daily transport activity into actionable insight, helping an operator see cash flow, route pressure, unpaid accounts, fuel cost signals, and trip volume.

Technologies Used

React, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Chart.js, Leaflet Maps, Local Storage, JSON, dashboard design, location intelligence.

Key Findings

  • Transport operations need linked data across students, routes, trips, payments, and expenses.
  • Cash flow visibility improves when unpaid accounts and trip income are visible in one place.
  • Route and fuel context can reveal operating pressure before it becomes a larger business issue.
  • Alerts help operators focus attention on missed trips, unpaid students, and high cost signals.

Business Impact

Insight Rides helps transport operators move from scattered records to a structured operating system. It supports better payment follow up, route monitoring, cost awareness, reporting, and day to day decision making.

What This Product Demonstrates

This product demonstrates operational analytics, interface planning, structured data design, local application state, dashboard reporting, location intelligence, and product development for a real business workflow.
