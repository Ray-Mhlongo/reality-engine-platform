# Cathdel Creamy Voiceover Script (South African Tone)

## 30s Voiceover (With Pacing Marks)
Use a warm, confident, local business tone.  
Legend: `/` short pause, `//` medium pause, `...` emphasis pause.

### Full Read
School break is short // and queues move fast.  
With Cathdel Creamy, you can scan or select a customer / and record sales in seconds.  
No paper books... no confusion... just fast checkout.  
Loyalty updates automatically // so repeat customers keep coming back.  
Track revenue, stock, and top products in real time / and know what to restock before you run out.  
Then send smart WhatsApp promos to the right customers // at the right time.  
Cathdel Creamy. // Sell faster. Reward more. Grow daily revenue.

## 15s Cutdown Voiceover
Queues are fast. // Sales should be faster.  
Cathdel Creamy helps you scan, sell, and track loyalty in seconds.  
See your numbers, send smart WhatsApp promos, and grow daily revenue.  
Cathdel Creamy. // Install. Sell. Grow.

## Delivery Notes
- Accent: neutral South African English.
- Pace target: ~130-145 words per minute.
- Keep energy rising from scene 1 to scene 6, then resolve calmly on the final brand line.
- Smile slightly on the final CTA so it sounds confident, not aggressive.
