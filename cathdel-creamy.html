Business management platform

Cathdel Creamy

A custom platform for managing a school based ice cream business.

Cathdel Creamy turns daily business activity into a structured workflow for customers, sales, attendance, promotions, reporting, invoices, QR functionality, and operational management.

Project Overview

Business problem: A small school based ice cream business needs a practical way to manage customers, track purchases, record attendance, handle promotions, generate reports, and keep operations organized without relying on scattered manual records.

Objective: Build a business management platform that supports everyday operations while also creating usable data for sales visibility, customer follow up, loyalty opportunities, and reporting.

Methodology: I structured the product around real business workflows: customer records, sales entries, attendance tracking, invoice support, QR functionality, promotion management, and reporting views.

Outcome: The product provides a central operational interface that helps the business understand what is happening daily and gives the owner a cleaner way to manage growth.

Technologies Used

Business application design, sales workflow planning, operational reporting, invoicing workflow, QR functionality, dashboard thinking.

Key Findings

  • Small business activity becomes easier to manage when customers, sales, and attendance are captured in one workflow.
  • Sales records can support better product performance, promotion, and repeat customer decisions.
  • Operational tools such as invoices and QR features reduce manual admin pressure.
  • A product interface can turn everyday activity into data that supports business decisions.

Business Impact

Cathdel Creamy helps a small business move from informal record keeping to a more scalable operating system. It supports clearer sales visibility, customer management, promotional planning, attendance tracking, and reporting.

What This Product Demonstrates

This product demonstrates product thinking, business workflow design, operational data capture, reporting design, automation awareness, and the ability to transform a real business need into a usable software concept.
