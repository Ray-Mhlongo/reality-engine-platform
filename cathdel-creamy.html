Small business sales and operations monitoring

Cathdel Creamy

A sales and operations monitoring solution for a school based ice cream business.

Cathdel Creamy structures customer purchasing records, sales transactions, inventory visibility, promotion monitoring, and daily reporting so small business activity can be tracked and reviewed consistently.

Project Overview

This project addresses the need for a small school based ice cream business to capture daily activity in a structured way instead of relying on scattered manual records.

The solution organizes customer records, sales entries, attendance-related activity, promotion tracking, invoice support, QR workflows, and reporting views into one operational monitoring process.

The business outcome is clearer visibility into daily sales activity, customer purchasing behavior, promotions, and operational follow-up needs.

Business Challenge

Small business owners often make decisions from memory, notebooks, or scattered spreadsheets. This makes it difficult to track which products are selling, which customers are active, and which promotions or invoices need attention.

The project needed to turn everyday operational activity into structured data that could support reporting and decision making.

Data Sources

The monitored data includes customer records, sales transactions, product activity, promotion records, invoice status, QR interactions, and operational workflow records.

These records support customer activity tracking, sales performance monitoring, promotion review, and daily operational reporting.

Data Processing and Analysis

I structured the workflow around repeatable sales and customer records so transactions could be reviewed by product, customer, promotion, and operational status.

The reporting logic supports performance monitoring, sales visibility, customer follow-up, and promotion analysis without inventing scale or claiming business impact that was not measured.

Tools and Technologies

Operational data collection, sales workflow planning, customer records, promotion monitoring, invoicing workflow, QR functionality, dashboard thinking.

Reporting and Insights

  • Small business activity becomes easier to manage when customers, sales, and attendance are captured in one workflow.
  • Sales records can support better product performance, promotion, and repeat customer decisions.
  • Operational tools such as invoices and QR features reduce manual admin pressure.
  • A product interface can turn everyday activity into data that supports business decisions.
